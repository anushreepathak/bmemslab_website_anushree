Biography

Supreeth Setty is a Ph.D. candidate in the MicroSystems and BioMEMS Laboratory at the University of Cincinnati, specializing in the development of immunodiagnostic lab-on-a-chip platforms for Point-of-Care Testing (POCT). His research focuses on integrating biosensing, microfluidics, and chemiluminescence-based detection to enhance the sensitivity and portability of diagnostic assays for acute, chronic, and infectious diseases. With a background in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) and expertise in MEMS, sensor technology, and biomedical device development, he aims to bridge the gap between academic research and real-world healthcare solutions.
